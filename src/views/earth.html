Earth | መሬት

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known astronomical object to harbor life.

Stats

Diameter: 12,742 km

Mass: 5.972 × 10^24 kg

Orbital period: 365.24 days

Distance from sun: 149.6 million km

Features

Earth's atmosphere is composed primarily of nitrogen and oxygen.

It has liquid water on its surface, which is essential for life as we know it.

Moons

Earth has one natural satellite, known as the Moon.

Moon: The Moon is the fifth largest satellite in the Solar System and is thought to have formed around 4.5 billion years ago.

Explorations

Earth has been extensively studied by both robotic spacecraft and humans.

Space Probes: Numerous space probes have been sent to study Earth's atmosphere, climate, geology, and oceans.

Space Stations: Space stations like the International Space Station (ISS) provide platforms for scientific research and observations of Earth from space.

Satellites: Thousands of satellites orbit Earth, providing data for weather forecasting, communications, navigation, and environmental monitoring.

Manned Missions: Human missions have allowed astronauts to observe Earth from space and conduct experiments on its surface.

Potential for Life

Earth is the only known planet to support life.

It is home to a diverse range of organisms, including plants, animals, and microorganisms.

Fun Facts

Earth's atmosphere extends up to about 10,000 kilometers above the surface.

The highest point on Earth is the summit of Mount Everest, reaching an elevation of 8,848 meters above sea level.
